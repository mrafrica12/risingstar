Official Guidelines

Tournament Rules

All Rising Stars Atlanta events are governed by these core regulations to ensure fair, safe, and competitive play for all participants.

Important Note

All rules are subject to review and update. The most current version is always available here. Questions? Contact us at info@risingstarsatlanta.com

01
Eligibility

Players must compete in their age-appropriate division. Valid ID or birth certificate required at check-in for U12 and above. No exceptions.

02
Team Size

5v5: minimum 5 players, maximum 8 on roster. 7v7: minimum 7 players, maximum 12. Rosters must be submitted 5 days before event.

03
Match Duration

5v5: Two 15-minute halves. 7v7: Two 20-minute halves. 5-minute halftime break. Knockout rounds may use golden goal format.

04
Equipment

Cleats required (no metal studs). Shin guards mandatory. Numbered bibs provided. No jewelry during play. Goalkeeper must wear different color.

05
Discipline

Yellow card: warning. Two yellows = red = player ejected for remainder of match. Red card direct = suspended for next match minimum. Zero tolerance for violence or abuse.

06
Scoring & Standings

Win: 3 points. Draw: 1 point. Loss: 0 points. Tiebreakers: goal difference, goals scored, head-to-head, penalty shootout.
