5v5 teams require 5–8 players. 7v7 teams require 7–12 players. Rosters must be finalized one week before the tournament.
Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. No refunds within 7 days of the event, but your registration can be transferred to another tournament.
Players need cleats (no metal studs), shin guards, and a water bottle. Team jerseys are provided in the entry fee. Goalkeepers need gloves.
Players may only compete in their age-appropriate division. Age verification (birth certificate or ID) may be required at check-in.
