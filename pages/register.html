Questions

Registration FAQs

When does the season start? +

The Summer 2026 season kicks off July 18, 2026 with the Summer Classic tournament. Regular weekly sessions begin the following Saturday, July 26. A full schedule will be sent to all registered families one week before the start date.

What is the refund policy? +

Cancellation and transfer details are reviewed with families during confirmation. Contact us before the registration deadline if plans change.

Can my child join mid-season? +

Yes, we accept mid-season registrations based on availability. Spots are limited and we close registration once teams are full. Contact us directly if you're looking to join mid-season.

What equipment does my player need? +

Players need: cleats (turf or firm ground), shin guards, and athletic shorts/socks. A water bottle and athletic wear are strongly recommended. The official Rising Stars jersey is provided as part of your registration. Visit our Resources page for the full equipment checklist.

When and how do I pay? +

Payment is collected after your registration is reviewed and approved by our staff. You'll receive a payment link via email within 24–48 hours of submitting this form. We accept all major credit/debit cards, Zelle, and Cash App. Your spot is not guaranteed until payment is received.

My child has never played before. Is that OK? +

Absolutely. We welcome players of all experience levels. Our U6 and U8 programs are specifically designed for beginners and focus on fun, fundamentals, and building a love for the game. Our coaches create an inclusive, encouraging environment where every child can thrive.

Can I register for multiple programs? +

Yes! Many families combine a league program (5v5 or 7v7) with private training sessions for accelerated development. Complete one registration form per program. If you're registering for both a league and a tournament, please submit separate forms. Contact us for multi-program discount options.
