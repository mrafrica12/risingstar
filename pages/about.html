Our Story

Built for the Next Level

Our Mission

We don't just train football players. We develop champions — in sport, in discipline, and in life.

Rising Stars Atlanta was founded on a simple belief: every youth athlete in Atlanta deserves access to elite football development. We built a program where technical excellence meets community, where competition meets character, and where every player — regardless of background — has the chance to compete at the highest level.

Since 2019, we have trained over 500 players, hosted 12 major tournaments, and watched our alumni go on to compete at high school, club, and collegiate levels across the Southeast.

Our Journey

From 2019
to Today

What started as a single 5v5 tournament has grown into Atlanta's most trusted elite youth football organization.

2019
Founded

Rising Stars Atlanta hosts its first tournament at a local Atlanta field with 12 teams and 80 players.

2020
Programs Launch

Elite 5v5 and 7v7 seasonal programs introduced. First cohort of 150 players enrolled across 6 age groups.

2022
Private Training

Personalized 1-on-1 coaching program launched. USSF-licensed coaching staff expanded to 6 coaches.

2023
500 Players

Milestone: 500 players trained. Summer Classic tournament attracts teams from across Georgia and neighboring states.

2026
Active Now
Next Chapter

Website relaunch, expanded tournament schedule, merchandise line, and new partnerships with elite clubs.
