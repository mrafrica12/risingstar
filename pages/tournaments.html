5v5 teams require 5–8 players. 7v7 teams require 7–12 players. Rosters must be finalized one week before the tournament date.
Players need cleats (no metal studs), shin guards, and a water bottle. Goalkeepers need gloves. Check the specific SincSports tournament page for jersey requirements per event.
