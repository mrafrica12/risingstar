Private Training

All Ages · All Positions

The fastest path to elite-level improvement. Private sessions are fully customized around each player's development goals — whether it's mastering a skill, improving fitness, or preparing for tryouts.

  • 1-on-1 or small group (2–4 players)
  • Fully customized training plan
  • Flexible scheduling — mornings, evenings, weekends
  • Position-specific technical coaching
  • Mental performance coaching included
  • Progress reports after every 4 sessions
