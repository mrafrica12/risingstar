1-on-1 Coaching

Private Training

All Ages · All Positions

The fastest path to elite-level improvement. Private sessions are fully customized around each player's development goals — whether it's mastering a skill, improving fitness, or preparing for tryouts.

  • 1-on-1 or small group (2–4 players)
  • Fully customized training plan
  • Flexible scheduling — mornings, evenings, weekends
  • Position-specific technical coaching
  • Mental performance coaching included
  • Progress reports after every 4 sessions
Availability confirmed by coaching staff

What We Focus On

Training Priorities

Every session is designed to develop the core abilities that separate elite players from the rest.

⚽

Technical Skills

Master the fundamentals of passing, receiving, and controlling the ball with precision under pressure.

🎯

Ball Mastery / Control

First touch development and ball-per-feet drills that build confidence and command over the ball.

🔥

Finishing

Clinical finishing techniques — shooting accuracy, composure in the box, and converting chances.

🧠

Tactical Instructions

Game intelligence — positioning, reading the game, and making smart decisions in real time.

⚡

Speed & Quickness

Explosive movement with and without the ball — acceleration, agility, and directional changes.

Elite Soccer Pickup

High-Level Competition · Competitive Play

Rising Stars Elite Soccer Pickup is an exclusive, invite-only program for the most competitive players. Players compete against elite peers in a high-intensity, game-focused environment designed to develop winning mentality and championship-level play.

  • Invite-only membership (invite code required)
  • Elite-level competitive matches
  • Tournament preparation focus
  • Coaching staff supervision
  • Player evaluation & feedback
  • Network with top players in Atlanta
